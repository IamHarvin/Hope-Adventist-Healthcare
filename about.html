Our Historical Operational Timeline

Hope Horizon Healthcare Arusha roots date back to 1972, initiated by dedicated medical missionaries setup to deliver fundamental critical therapeutic interventions locally.

The system evolved progressively through critical regulatory updates, establishing formal registration operations under governing frameworks to construct localized clinical pathways.

1972

Establishment

Inaugural clinic deployments initiated by German missionary groups in Arusha.

2020

Active

Providing functional secondary specialist referral configurations inside East Africa.
